About HUGPONG

HUGPONG is an integrated platform designed to bridge the gap between farm owners, supervisors, and field workers. By providing real-time market price monitoring, advanced profitability calculations, and robust offline-first operational scheduling, HUGPONG empowers the Silay agricultural community to make data-driven decisions and streamline harvest workflows.
