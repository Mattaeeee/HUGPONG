About HUGPONG

HUGPONG is an offline-first agricultural management platform designed to streamline sugarcane block farm operations. It empowers Farm Managers and SRA (Admin) to track the strict 8-stage crop cycle, monitor weekly SRA prices, and generate certified audit reports—all from a single, unified console. Built to survive intermittent field connectivity and ensure administrative transparency, HUGPONG bridges the gap between manual farm-level data and secure regional oversight.
